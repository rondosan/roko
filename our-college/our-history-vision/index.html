    Our History and Vision

    Our History

    Regent College London have a rich and successful history within education. Regent College London is a part of Regent Group, an education, training and investment management business that was developed by the Pankaj family in 2000. Regent Group has gone from strength to strength over the many years and established Regent College London in 2010. The college was initially delivering BTEC Higher National Diplomas and providing students with alternative progression routes into Higher Education.

    In 2013 we opened admissions to the public to reflect our commitment to widening access and participation. Since then we have been delivering a range of HND business programmes, validated by Pearson. We have expanded our Higher Education provision, working with four university partners to deliver Foundation year routes, Foundation degrees, Bachelor’s degrees, Top-up degrees and Masters degrees in a range of subjects.

    Our “family business” has grown at a rapid pace and now includes a range of educational institutions and businesses. Regent College London has been at the forefront of our success as we help many students achieve lifelong goals and aspirations. We started with our very first college in Wembley, which has gone onto becoming our biggest campus location, home to over a 1000 students! Since 2010, we have developed college locations in Kingsbury, Southall, Central London, Harrow and of course, Wembley.

    As a college, we have achieved many goals such as a positive outcome for QAA Higher Education Review, excellent results in National Student Survey, opened 5 campuses across London and of course, helped many students achieve their goals and aspirations. We will continue to thrive and provide the upmost best to our students!

     

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to be the UK’s first truly local London University with a global focus, a global brand and global campuses from 2025 to 2030.

    From our campus locations, and globally via our Regent Digital, we will provide learners with an outstanding education and student experience underpinned by the philosophy of Napoleon Hill and Andrew Carnegie, characterised by compact campuses, small classes and individualised support.

    Partnerships

    Working alongside leading universities in the UK

    Regent College London is proud to have partnered with established universities to help students achieve the degree of their dreams. Each of the Foundation Degrees, Foundation Year Routes, Bachelor’s Degrees and Top-Up Degrees offered at Regent College London is validated and awarded by one of our partner institutions. Like us, they put students at the heart of everything they do. Their focus on making a positive difference to every student perfectly matches the ethos of Regent College London. We are very proud to work with each partner institution.

    Governance and External Expertise

    The Board of Governors of Regent College London are the College’s directors, Dr Selva Pankaj, Mrs Tharshiny Pankaj, Dr Maurizio Bragagni and Mr Simon Blagden CBE. Dr Selva Pankaj also acts as the College Principal. The Board of Governors holds ultimate responsibility for the management of all aspects of the operation of Regent College London. The Board of Governors has three sub-committees reporting into it including the Finance Committee, Audit and Risk Committee and the Nominations and Remuneration Committee.

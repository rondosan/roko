    School of Allied Health

    The Regent College London School of Allied Health located in London captures the significance of this vital industry; providing students with the essence of learning its complexities.

    The School of Allied Health located in London plays a pivotal role in helping students understand the fundamentals of the Health and Social industries. We offer Health and Social Care and Health and Social Science courses at different levels, allowing students of all levels to enrol enhance their skills, knowledge and wellbeing. Our courses are designed to help students draw on many different aspects of the industries such as Psychology, Sociology, Public Health and much more! If you want to make a difference within the world of Health and Social Care, our excellent standard of teaching and highly qualified tutors will help get you there. We also offer flexible learning, helping you fit your studies around busy life commitments!
